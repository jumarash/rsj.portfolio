About me

I'm Rashid And I'm an Interaction Designer

I am currently working towards an advanced diploma in interaction design at George Brown College. If I had to describe myself, I would say I am curious. I am outgoing and a team player. I love networking with people. It gives me a chance to pick their brains and understand how they solve problems. I see it as an opportunity to learn new things and also as a chance to improve myself as a professional and put things into practice when I am creating my design solutions.

My goal is to design products, user experiences and interfaces that have meaningful and impactful to users. I am a passionate designer that always wants to grow, learn and try new things. I love new challenges that are difficult to come up with a solution. It forces me to view things from different perspectives to find better and unique solutions.

Experienced Food Service Worker with a demonstrated history of working in the higher education industry. Skills in 3D Visualization, User Experience (UX), Visual Design, Product Design, Web Design, and Interaction Design. Strong operations professional with an Advanced Diploma focused in Interaction Design from George Brown College.
